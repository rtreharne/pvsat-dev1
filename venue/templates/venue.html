Award Winning Facilities

PVSAT-12 will be held at UoL's Central Teaching Hub (CTH). The building - purpose built for our undergraduate teaching in Chemistry, Physics, Environmental Sciences and Archaeology - is a perfect conference venue, with open atriums for exhibitors and poster presentations as well as a suite of state of the art lecture theatres.

World Class Energy Research

The Stephenson Institute for Renewable Energy (located directly opposite the CTH) is dedicated to exploring the future of renewable, clean and sustainable energy technology. A primary focus of this research is thin-film photovoltaics which is led by Prof. Ken Durose's group. We look forward to showing you around our research labsi.

CDT-PV

UoL is the lead co-ordinator of the Center for Doctoral Training in New and Sustainable Photovoltaics. The CDT will train over 60 PhD researchers with a curriculum dedicated to PV. Our first two cohorts will be attending PVSAT-12. Please don't be afraid to say hello to this group of highly enthusiastic and well informed researchers.
